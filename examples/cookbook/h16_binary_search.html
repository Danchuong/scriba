Step 1 / 11

Tim kiem nhi phan tren A=[1,3,4,6,7,9,11,13,15] (da sap xep tang dan, n=9). Muc tieu: tim target=7 trong O(logn). Quy uoc: lo la bien trai, hi la bien phai (ca hai cung dong), mid=(lo+hi)/2 la chi so giua. Vong lap ket thuc khi lo>hi hoac tim thay A[mid]=target.

Step 2 / 11

Vong 1 (tim 7): lo=0, hi=8, mid=(0+8)/2=4. Ca so luong phan tu va mid deu ro: ca so [0,8] phu toan bo mang.

Step 3 / 11

Kiem tra A[4]=7 vs target=7. Trung! Tra ve chi so 4. Truong hop tot nhat gap ngay o lan so sanh dau tien.

Step 4 / 11

Tim thay target=7 tai chi so 4. Tong so phep so sanh: 1. De minh hoa day du qua trinh thu hep cua so, ta chay them mot vi du khong may man: tim target=8 tren cung mang -- 8 khong nam trong A.

Step 5 / 11

Reset. Vong 1 (tim 8): lo=0, hi=8, mid=4. So sanh A[4]=7 voi 8. Vi 7<8, target phai nam o nua phai -- loai nua trai va chinh mid.

Step 6 / 11

Vong 2: lo←mid+1=5, hi=8, mid=(5+8)/2=6. Cua so con [5,8] co 4 phan tu. So sanh A[6]=11 voi 8. Vi 11>8, target (neu ton tai) phai nam o nua trai cua mid -- loai nua phai va chinh mid.

Step 7 / 11

Vong 3: lo=5, hi←mid−1=5, mid=(5+5)/2=5. Cua so con chi gom mot phan tu duy nhat: A[5]=9. So sanh 9 voi 8: 9>8 -- loai nua phai.

Step 8 / 11

Vong 4: lo=5, hi←mid−1=4. Vi lo=5>hi=4, pham vi tim kiem rong -- thoat vong lap. Tong cong 3 phep so sanh, phu hop voi ⌈log2​9⌉=4 ranh gioi.

Step 9 / 11

Ket luan: 8 nam giua A[4]=7 va A[5]=9 -- khong ton tai trong mang, tra ve −1. Moi vong lap cat doi pham vi con lai, nen so vong lap toi da la ⌈log2​n⌉+1. Truy vet (lo,hi,mid) qua cac vong: (0,8,4)→(5,8,6)→(5,5,5)→lo>hi.

Step 10 / 11

Luu y cai dat: su dung mid=lo+(hi−lo)/2 thay cho (lo+hi)/2 de tranh tran so nguyen khi lo va hi lon (bug noi tieng cua \texttt{java.util.Arrays.binarySearch} truoc Java 1.6). Cac bien the pho bien: \texttt{lower\_bound} tra ve chi so dau tien co A[i]≥target, \texttt{upper\_bound} tra ve chi so dau tien co A[i]>target.

Step 11 / 11

Binh luan chung: binary search yeu cau mang da sap xep va mot quan he thu tu nhat quan. Lua chon bien [lo,hi] (dong ca hai dau, thoat khi lo>hi) nhu o day la mau pho bien; lua chon nua mo [lo,hi) cung thong dung voi dieu kien thoat lo≥hi -- chon mot va dung nhat quan trong toan bo code base.
