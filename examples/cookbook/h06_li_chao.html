Step 1 / 8

Li Chao Tree quan ly tap cac duong thang y = ax + b. Ho tro them duong va truy van min/max tai x. Minh hoa chen 6 duong va tim bao loi duoi (lower envelope).

Step 2 / 8

Chen L1: y = 1.5x + 1. Duong dau tien, tu dong la bao loi duoi tren toan mien [0,8].

Step 3 / 8

Chen L2: y = -0.5x + 10. Cat L1 tai x = 4.5. L2 nho hon ben phai, L1 nho hon ben trai. Bao loi cap nhat.

Step 4 / 8

Chen L3: y = 0.8x + 2. L3 nam duoi L1 tren [0, ~1.4] nhung tren L1 sau do. Li Chao chia doan [0,8] tai mid=4 de quyet dinh giu duong nao.

Step 5 / 8

Chen L4: y = 0.2x + 3. Duong gan ngang. Tao doan bao loi moi quanh x=2..5 noi L4 thap hon L1 va L2.

Step 6 / 8

Chen L5: y = -x + 11. Do doc am manh. Thap nhat tai x=8 (y=3). Chiem bao loi o doan phai.

Step 7 / 8

Chen L6: y = 2x. Di qua goc toa do. Thap nhat tai x=0 (y=0) nhung tang nhanh. Chi chiem bao loi o x rat nho.

Step 8 / 8

Bao loi duoi: L6 (x nho), L4 (x giua), L5 (x lon). Truy van min tai bat ky x trong O(log n). Li Chao Tree la ky thuat manh cho DP Convex Hull Trick!
