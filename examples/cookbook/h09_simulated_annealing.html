Step 1 / 9

Simulated Annealing (SA) giai bai TSP 6 thanh pho. Bat dau voi tour A-B-C-D-E-F-A. Nhiet do cao: chap nhan loi di xau. Nhiet do ha: chi nhan loi di tot.

Step 2 / 9

Tour ban dau: A-&gt;B-&gt;C-&gt;D-&gt;E-&gt;F-&gt;A. Chi phi = 42. Nhiet do T=100 (cao).

Step 3 / 9

Buoc 1: Dao doan [C,D] -&gt; tour A-B-D-C-E-F-A. Chi phi giam 42-&gt;38. Chap nhan! T=80.

Step 4 / 9

Buoc 2: Thu dao doan [B..E] -&gt; chi phi tang 38-&gt;41. T=64 con cao, xac suat chap nhan = e^(-3/64) = 0.95. Chap nhan de thoat cuc tri!

Step 5 / 9

Buoc 3: Dao doan [D..F] -&gt; chi phi giam manh 41-&gt;35. Chap nhan. T=51. Dang tien gan loi giai tot.

Step 6 / 9

Buoc 4: Dao doan [D,E] -&gt; chi phi 35-&gt;33. Cai thien. T=41.

Step 7 / 9

Buoc 5: Thu dao khac -&gt; chi phi tang 33-&gt;36. T=33, xac suat = e^(-3/33) = 0.91. Van chap nhan -- nhiet do chua du thap.

Step 8 / 9

Buoc 6: Dao thanh cong -&gt; chi phi giam 36-&gt;31. T=26. Dang hoi tu.

Step 9 / 9

Buoc 7-8: T thap, chi chap nhan cai thien. Chi phi toi uu = 29. MetricPlot cho thay cost giam dan va temp ha theo lich trinh. SA tim loi giai gan toi uu cho NP-hard!
