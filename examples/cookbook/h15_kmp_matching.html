Step 1 / 22

KMP tim mau P = ABABCABAB trong chuoi T = ABABDABACDABABCABAB. Hai pha: (1) dung bang that bai F[i] tren P, (2) duyet T mot lan, dung F de nhay lui j khi khong khop -- khong bao gio lui i.

Step 2 / 22

Pha 1 -- xay F. Quy uoc F[0]=0: khong co tien to that su nao co the la hau to cua chinh ky tu dau.

Step 3 / 22

i=1: so sanh P[1]= B voi P[0]= A. Khac nhau, khong co tien to nao trung -- F[1]=0.

Step 4 / 22

i=2: P[2]= A = P[0]= A. Do dai tien to trung dai nhat tang tu 0 len 1 -- F[2]=1.

Step 5 / 22

i=3: mo rong tien to dai 1 -- so sanh P[3]= B voi P[F[2]]=P[1]= B. Khop, do dai len 2 -- F[3]=2.

Step 6 / 22

i=4: P[4]= C vs P[F[3]]=P[2]= A. Khong khop -- lui j←F[1]=0, thu lai P[4] vs P[0]= A: van lech. j da ve 0, dung -- F[4]=0.

Step 7 / 22

i=5: P[5]= A = P[0]= A. F[5]=1.

Step 8 / 22

i=6: P[6]= B = P[1]= B. F[6]=2.

Step 9 / 22

i=7: P[7]= A = P[2]= A. F[7]=3.

Step 10 / 22

i=8: P[8]= B = P[3]= B. F[8]=4. Bang that bai hoan tat: F=[0,0,1,2,0,1,2,3,4].

Step 11 / 22

Pha 2 -- quet T. Con tro i chay tren T, j chay tren P. Khi khop: ca hai tien. Khi lech: j←F[j−1] (khong bao gio lui i).

Step 12 / 22

i=0,j=0: T[0]= A = P[0]= A. Ca hai tien.

Step 13 / 22

Bon vong khop lien tiep ABAB. Bay gio i=3,j=3 va T[3]= B = P[3]= B -- chuan bi kiem T[4].

Step 14 / 22

i=4,j=4: T[4]= D vs P[4]= C. Lech! Lui j←F[j−1]=F[3]=2, giu i=4.

Step 15 / 22

j=2: thu lai T[4]= D vs P[2]= A. Van lech -- j←F[1]=0.

Step 16 / 22

j=0: T[4]= D vs P[0]= A. Lech, j da 0 -- tien i, giu j=0.

Step 17 / 22

i=5,j=0: T[5]= A = P[0]= A. Bat dau khop lai.

Step 18 / 22

Khop them ABA. Bay gio i=8,j=3: T[8]= C vs P[3]= B. Lech! j←F[2]=1.

Step 19 / 22

j=1: T[8]= C vs P[1]= B. Lech -- j←F[0]=0.

Step 20 / 22

j=0: T[8]= C vs P[0]= A. Lech, tien i, giu j=0.

Step 21 / 22

i=9: D vs A, lech, tien i. i=10,j=0: T[10]= A = P[0]. Bat dau lai. Lan nay se la phat an: tu i=10 toan bo 9 ky tu T[10..18] se khop voi P[0..8].

Step 22 / 22

Khop toan bo P voi T[10..18]. KMP tim thay mau tai vi tri 10, tong cong O(∣T∣+∣P∣) phep so sanh -- con tro i khong bao gio lui lai.
