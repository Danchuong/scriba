Step 1 / 7

FFT bien doi day [a0..a7] thanh [A0..A7] bang cach de quy chia doi. Vong tron don vi phia tren cho thay 8 can bac 8 cua 1: w^k = e^(2*pi*i*k/8).

Step 2 / 7

Buoc 1: Chia thanh chi so chan (a0,a2,a4,a6) va chi so le (a1,a3,a5,a7). FFT de quy tren moi nua.

Step 3 / 7

Buoc 2: FFT nua chan cho E[k] = FFT(a0,a2,a4,a6). Voi N/2=4, dung w4 = can bac 4 cua 1.

Step 4 / 7

Buoc 3: FFT nua le cho O[k] = FFT(a1,a3,a5,a7). Tuong tu voi w4.

Step 5 / 7

Butterfly k=0: A[0] = E[0] + w8^0 * O[0], A[4] = E[0] - w8^0 * O[0]. Twiddle factor w8^0 = 1.

Step 6 / 7

Butterfly k=1,2,3: A[k] = E[k] + w8^k * O[k], A[k+4] = E[k] - w8^k * O[k]. Twiddle factor w8^k quay tren vong tron don vi.

Step 7 / 7

Hoan tat! FFT tinh [A0..A7] trong O(n log n) = 8*3 = 24 phep tinh, thay vi O(n^2) = 64. Day la nen tang cua nhan da thuc nhanh.
